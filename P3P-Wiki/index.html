The Midnight Hour Awaits

Persona 3 Portable is the definitive portable edition of one of the most beloved JRPGs ever made. Released for PlayStation Portable, it introduced an entirely new Female Protagonist route — giving players the chance to experience the Dark Hour through a fresh perspective with unique social links and story beats. The 2023 remaster brought this enhanced version to PC and modern consoles.

🌑

The Dark Hour

A hidden 25th hour occurring each midnight, invisible to ordinary people who transform into coffins. Only Persona-users remain conscious, while the monsters called Shadows roam freely through this twisted reality.

🗼

Tartarus

A massive tower manifesting every Dark Hour, formed from Gekkoukan High School itself. SEES must ascend through hundreds of procedurally generated floors, battling Shadows to uncover the mystery behind the Dark Hour.

🌕

Full Moon Operations

Each month, a powerful Shadow boss manifests during the Full Moon. These high-stakes fixed story events advance the narrative — all twelve must be cleared to reach the true ending of the game.

🎴

Social Links

The heart of Persona. Building bonds with characters representing Tarot Arcana strengthens your ability to create more powerful Personas through fusion. Maxing Social Links is crucial to unlocking the true ending.

⚔️

Persona System

Characters awaken to Personas — manifestations of their inner selves — by pointing an Evoker at their head. The Wild Card protagonist can hold multiple Personas simultaneously, switching freely in battle.

👤

Two Routes

P3P offers two protagonists. The Male route follows the original story. The Female route features unique Social Links, romance options with Shinjiro and Ryoji, and a different emotional tone throughout the journey.
